How to enter grammar?

  • One line — one rule
  • Rule consists of two parts: left and right
  • Left and right parts are separated by "->"
  • Left part can contain only one nonterminal
  • Right part can contain terminals and nonterminals or empty chain
  • Terminals and nonterminals in rule's right part must be separated by space
  • Empty chain must contain only one character — ε
